## ğŸ”µ What is BlueCarbon Ledger?

### Key Concept: What is Blue Carbon?

- ğŸŒŠ **Mangroves** - sequester up to 10x more carbon than terrestrial forests
- ğŸŒ± **Seagrass Meadows** - store carbon in sediments for millennia
- ğŸ§‚ **Salt Marshes** - tidal wetlands with exceptional carbon capture
